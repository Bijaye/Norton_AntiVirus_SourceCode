Purchasing - Book Buying

Description:
  • Books may be purchased by regular IBM employees of the Watson Research Center for private departmental use by contacting Complete Book and Media Supply, Inc. (CBMSI)
How to Obtain Service:
  • Contact Complete Book and Media Supply, Inc. (CBMSI) by:
    • Phone: 800-986-1775
    • FAX: 512-249-0720
    • EMAIL: (Lotus Notes) books@cbmsi.com@internet
    • EMAIL: (VM) books at cbmsi.com
Please provide the following information to CBMSI:
  • Your name, department number, phone/fax number, Watson Vice President
  • Quantity required, book title, book ISBN number
  • If ISBN is not available:
    • Provide as much additional information as possible (ie. author, publisher, edition, soft/hardcover, publication date).
Additional Information:
  • Within 24 hours of your order, you will receive:
    • Confirmation by FAX or EMAIL that your order has been placed
    • The price and expected delivery date
  • You will not receive an invoice.
    • Blanket orders have been set up to cover all departments, charging to the Watson Vice President.
    • Your department's financial analyst will receive monthly reports on books purchased.
If you are not an employee of the TJWRC:
  • Do not call CBMSI to order your books via the blanket orders.
  • Please pay for books on a personal credit card and obtain reimbursement via an MEA (Miscellaneous Expense Account).
  • For book purchases over $500, please submit a requisition using CPOPS.
Purchasing Interface:
  • Regina Glownia - 24-247C, 8-862-3867, rglownia @ ibmus
