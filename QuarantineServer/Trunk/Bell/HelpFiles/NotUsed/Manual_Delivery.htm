Manual delivery of virus definitions

The Analysis Center can be manually queried for virus signature definitions files for selected items in the quarantine.

To manually query for virus definintions for selected files:

  1. In the Symantec AntiVirus Quarantine, select one or more files.
  2. On the button bar, click the button.

To be eligible for manual definitions delivery:

  • The sample cannot already be eligible for automatic delivery of virus definitions (X-Signatures-Priority is 0).

  • The sample requires new definitions (X-Signatures-Sequence > 0).

  • The sample has not yet been disinfected (X-Date-Sample-Finished is missing or 0).
