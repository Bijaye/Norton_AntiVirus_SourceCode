AVIS Actions

The AVIS Actions tab reports sample submission and virus definition delivery status for the selected item in the Symantec AntiVirus quarantine.

You can manually queue a sample for submission to the analysis center, as well as query the gateway for updated virus definitions files for the selected sample.

If desired, you can override the default policy settings of the AVIS Policy tab of the Quarantine.

To specify AVIS Actions:

  1. In the left pane of the Symantec System Center console, right-click Symantec AntiVirus Quarantine.
  2. In the right pane, right-click a quarantined item, choose Properties, and click the AVIS Actions tab.
