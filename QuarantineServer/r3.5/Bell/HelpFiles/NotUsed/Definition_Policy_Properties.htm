Definition Policy

AVIS Policy settings determine default behavior for virus sample submissions to the gateway and the return of new virus signature definitions packages.

Policy settings can be superceded on an item-by-item basis when viewing the AVIS Actions property tab for a selected item in the Quarantine.

For automatic submission of samples, the Initial submission priority must be non-zero. Similarly, if Automatic delivery of definitions is checked, new virus definitions are sent automatically to the Symantec AntiVirus server when available.

To specify AVIS Policy:

  1. In the left pane of the Symantec System Center console, right-click Symantec AntiVirus Quarantine.

  2. Choose Properties and click the AVIS Policy tab.
