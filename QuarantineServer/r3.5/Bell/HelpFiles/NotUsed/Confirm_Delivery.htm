Definition Delivery Confirmation

The Definition Delivery Confirmation dialog box lists all samples selected in the Quarantine at the time the button was clicked. Click OK to initiate delivery of virus signature definitions files for the selected items.

After delivery, each items's X-Signature-Priority attribute is set to a non-zero value.

Grayed out items in the list did not meet the manual definition delivery criteria and cannot currently be retreived.

To be eligible for manual definitions delivery:

  • The sample cannot already be eligible for automatic delivery of virus definitions (X-Signatures-Priority is 0).

  • The sample requires new definitions (X-Signatures-Sequence > 0).

  • The sample has not yet been disinfected (X-Date-Sample-Finished is missing or 0).
