Installing Norton AntiVirus on Windows 95/98/Me client computers

Norton AntiVirus Corporate Edition protects the files on your computer and your company's network from computer viruses. Norton AntiVirus Corporate Edition runs in the background, providing you with continuous protection without interfering with your productivity.

Client requirements for installing Norton AntiVirus Corporate Edition

This program downloads and installs Norton AntiVirus to Windows 95, Windows 98 and Windows Me client computers. You must use Internet Explorer version 4.0 or higher. If you need assistance, please contact your system administrator or help desk staff.
