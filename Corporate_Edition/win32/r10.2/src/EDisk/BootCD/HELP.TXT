Your computer has the ability to start from a CD-ROM, and has started from
the CD containing Symantec Antivirus. You can use this CD as a Symantec Antivius
Emergency Disk.

- To use the Symantec Antivirus Emergency Disk programs, press Ctrl+Alt+Del to 
  restart your computer, then choose the first option.
- To start Windows normally, remove the CD containing Symantec Antivirus from 
  your CD-ROM drive, then press Ctrl+Alt+Del to restart your computer.
